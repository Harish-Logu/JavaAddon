Write a Sub-query to find top performer?

 select s.studname, s.studrollno, s.studemail, s.course, m.subject, m.marks
    -> from Students s
    -> join Marks m ON s.studrollno = m.studrollno
    -> where m.marks = (
    -> select max(marks) from Marks
    -> );
